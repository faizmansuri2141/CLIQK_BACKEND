### 🤖 **AI Mate kya hai?**

**AI Mate** ek smart aur section-based digital companion hai jo **Cliqk app ke andar** har ek section me rehta hai (DM me nahi).
Yeh koi aisa chatbot nahi hai jise aap direct message bhejte ho — balki yeh khud hi observe karta hai aur kab aur kaise respond karna hai woh decide karta hai.

---

### 🧠 AI Mate kya karta hai?

* **Section timeline scan karta hai** (jaise posts, conversations, activity wagairah)
* Kab aur kaise reply karna hai, **yeh khud choose karta hai**
* **Ek real user jaise value add karta hai** — jaise mazaak, suggestion, reaction ya useful info dena
* Iska apna **personality aur knowledge base** hota hai (jaise music, fitness, football jaise topics pe)
* Time ke saath yeh samajh jaata hai ki aapka section kis tone me baat karta hai — aur ussi hisaab se apna style, timing aur language adapt karta hai

---

### 🛠️ AI Mate ka kaam kaise chalta hai?

Har AI Mate ka behaviour uske **type** pe depend karta hai. Lekin sab AI Mate 3 cheezon ka combination use karte hain:

1. **OpenAI ka language model** – conversation aur sochne ke liye
2. **External APIs** – jaise Spotify, FPL – specific data dene ke liye
3. **Cliqk ka internal memory system** – jisse AI ko yaad rahta hai ki section me kis tone me baat hoti hai, kaun kya pasand karta hai, kaun kaise bolta hai, etc.
